Real Small Mario (Super Mario 2 1998 Hack)

This hack changes the small Mario/Luigi sprites to be more accurate to the original Super Mario All-Stars. It also corrects other small things such as Mario and Luigi's shoe colors and Fire Mario.

Note: You will need to patch a Super Mario 2 1998 ROM using Flips in order to play the hack. (This will NOT work with Sonic Jam 6)
